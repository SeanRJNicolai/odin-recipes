Anime Meat Recipe

This humongous meat shank is something out of an anime ... literally.

Ingredients

  • 150 grams thinly-sliced beef
  • 1 stick gobo (burdock root) , cut into 15 centimeters thickness

Meat

  • 400 grams ground meat
  • 80 grams chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • Potato starch

Sauce

  • 50cc sake
  • 30cc soy sauce
  • 30cc mirin
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon grated garlic

Directions

Anime Meat

  1. Peel, cut and shape burdock root to the size of a bone.
  2. Combine ingredients for the meat in a bowl and mix until combined.
  3. Transfer meat onto a sheet of plastic wrap and place the burdock on top. Wrap the meat around the burdock stick.
  4. Wrap the thinly-sliced beef around the ground beef mixture.
  5. Sprinkle with potato starch.
  6. Heat some oil in a frying pan and cook the meat on all sides over medium heat until nicely browned.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and turn heat to low. Steam for 30 minutes or until meat is done.

Sauce

  1. in ingredients for the sauce and cook until slightly thickened. Transfer to a plate and enjoy.
